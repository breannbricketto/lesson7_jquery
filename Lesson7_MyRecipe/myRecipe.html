Smores

Ingredients

  • 1 box of graham crackers
  • 3 Hershey's chocolate bars
  • 1 bag of jumbo marshmellows

Instructions

Preheat oven to 425.

Break graham crackers in half

Place half of them onto a baking sheet

Place a piece of chocolate onto each graham cracker

Place marshmellows on top of chocolate

Put in the heated oven and bake for 5 minutes

Take them out and place other half of graham cracker on top of marshmellow

Enjoy!
